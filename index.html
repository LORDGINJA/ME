Short Autobiography

I was born February 25, 1999 in Pasadena, California. I have lived here in Pasadena my whole life, though I have traveled to many places, including Germany, Italy, Mexico, and various states. I went to Hamilton Elementary School for Pre-K to 5th grade, then Wilson Middle School for 6th though 8th grade, and finally PHS, where I am currently in the 10th grade. At PHS, I am enrolled in the APP Academy.

Interests, Hobbies, Things I Hate, etc.

I like to consider myself a well-rounded person. I like education, but I also like sports and other outdoor activities and T.V. and other indoor activities.
